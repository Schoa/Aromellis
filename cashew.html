Cashew

Best in class product



WW180

Cashews of the largest size among Vietnamese varieties. It refers to approximately 180 cashew nuts per pound (0.45 kg).



WW210

WW210: Cashews with a smaller size compared to W180, with around 210 cashew nuts per pound.



WW240

W240: This is a popular medium-sized cashew nut, with approximately 240 cashew nuts per pound.



WW320

W320 cashews are also widely consumed, having a medium size, with around 320 cashew nuts per pound.



WW450

W450: The smallest-sized cashews among the classifications, with approximately 450 cashew nuts per pound.



Cashew Nuts WS

Cashew nut WS grade is a cashew nut kernel split naturally lengthwise and is known internationally as White Splits Cashew Nuts Kernel.



Cashew Nuts SP

Cashew nut SP grade (or: Small Pieces cashew, cashew small pieces) is a cashew nut that is broken into small pieces, internationally as SP cashew.



Cashew Nuts LP

Cashew nut LP grade (or: Large Pieces cashew, cashew large pieces, cashew LP, LP cashew) is a cashew nut that is broken into large pieces, internationally as LP cashew.



Cashew Nuts BB

Cashew nut BB grade (or: cashew baby bits pieces, white butts cashew, cashew white butts, cashew white butts nuts, cashew WB) is a cashew nut that is broken into very small pieces, internationally as BB cashew.



Cashew Nuts SW1

Cashew nut SW1 is a large, scorched, whole-grain cashew that have all the other characteristics of white kernels and has the same nutritional qualities, and is known internationally as SW cashew.



Cashew Nuts DW

Cashew nut DW grade is a cashew nut that may be slightly shriveled, immature, or maybe brown speckled or blemished on the surface, provided that dark brown, blue, > 60% rate on the surface



Cashew Nuts SK

Cashew nut that Black, brown, or other colored spots in aggregate over 1mm caused by the pre-harvest attack on the kernel,at least 1 colored spot or 2/3 light spots appear on the outer kernels surface



Cashew nuts WB

Cashew nut WB grade is a cashew nut kernel broken in crosswise and is known internationally as Butts Cashew Nuts Kernel.
