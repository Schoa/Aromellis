OUR PRODUCT

Pattern

Goofy Stripe is a pattern in which vivid colors of red, green, blue, and black harmonize in a stripe form. By arranging the proportions of each color bar differently, you can feel the light rhythm. It becomes a point in the space with retro color and atmosphere.

LittleThing

In the case of a cotton pouch, the pattern on one side is reversed due to the nature of the manufacturing method. In the case of a design with a large pattern unit, the pattern of all products is different depending on the cutting location.
